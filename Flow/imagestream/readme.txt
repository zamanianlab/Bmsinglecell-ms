Imaging flow cytometry run completed on 11/10/2021 with B. malayi mf single cell suspensions stained for viability (DRAQ5/Calcein-AM)

Raw images are located on Research Drive > ImageStream 

Dim and Full stain images were both used for analysis and only differ in concentrations of DRAQ5 and Calcein-AM.


Channel Key:
1 - Brightfield
2 - GFP (Calcein-AM dye)
5 - RFP (DRAQ5 dye)
6 - centering bead
